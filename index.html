Milestone Events & Impactful Moments

Sands of Change

In 2023, we met Rajesh, a skilled worker crafting beautiful diyas and decor from sand. His work was incredible, but his reach was limited. We helped him showcase his art at local markets, and soon his sand creations were flying off the shelves! Now, he’s turning grains of sand into a flourishing business and a brighter future.

Holi Stall Glow-Up!

In 2022, we found Aunty Gita struggling with her tucked-away Holi stall. We moved her to a prime spot, and suddenly, the crowd couldn’t get enough of her vibrant powders! What started as a quiet corner turned into a buzzing festival of colors and joy!

From Cobbler to Community Hero!

We met Ramu, a humble shoe cobbler with a toolbox and dreams bigger than his corner. In 2023, we helped him set up his very own shop in a busy market. Fast forward, and his shop is buzzing! Now, he’s not just fixing shoes; he’s fixing his future, one sole at a time!

Filling Empty Stomachs

In 2023, we launched a food donation initiative to provide warm meals to underprivileged communities. Over 1,000 meals were distributed across various regions, offering not just nourishment but also a sense of hope for those in need.

Strength in Sisterhood

In 2024, we launched a women empowerment program focusing on training women in skills like tailoring, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship. We've helped over 200 women start their own businesses, giving them the independence and confidence to lead change.

Empowering the Next Generation of Leaders

The annual RiseUp Conference, launched in 2022, is a platform where young leaders come together with entrepreneurs, investors, and changemakers to discuss and drive social impact. Over 1,000 students, professionals, and social leaders have attended this flagship event.
